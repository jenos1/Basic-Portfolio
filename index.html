About Me

I grew up in the Midwest and moved to New England after college. I attended graduate school at the University of New Hampshire, pursuing a career as a speech pathologist. After working in the field for some time, I became interested in computer technology and worked for many years in software quality assurance.

I have a musical side, and have played fiddle -- mostly bluegrass and old time music -- for over ten years. I have two Border Terrier dogs, and enjoy showing them in AKC conformation as well as in Earthdog trials.
